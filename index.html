How does Analytics help?

Many agencies claim to use Analytics, but simply look at visitor numbers.

We use Analytics to identify how your users are navigating your website to see if this is how the design intended. Identifying trends, geogaphical locations, advanced website usage goals and user information (non-personal GDPR compliant), data drives all the decisions we make.

I just need social media marketing

Ask yourself why. It may be to generate more bookings or enquiries, but seldom does a social media advert do this alone. It leads to somewhere else (your website). Is the whole process up to scratch in order to increase enquiries?
